Our Tour Packages

3-Day Maasai Mara Safari

Highlights: Big Five game drives, scenic savannahs, Maasai culture.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Nairobi – Maasai Mara, afternoon game drive.
  • Day 2: Full-day game drive with picnic lunch.
  • Day 3: Morning game drive, return to Nairobi.

5-Day Amboseli & Tsavo Safari

Highlights: Views of Mt. Kilimanjaro, large elephant herds, Tsavo wilderness.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Nairobi – Amboseli, afternoon safari.
  • Day 2: Full-day Amboseli exploration.
  • Day 3: Amboseli – Tsavo West, game drives.
  • Day 4: Tsavo East safari, Mzima Springs visit.
  • Day 5: Return to Nairobi.

7-Day Kenya Explorer

Highlights: Maasai Mara, Lake Nakuru, Mt. Kenya, Samburu.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Nairobi city tour.
  • Day 2: Nairobi – Samburu safari.
  • Day 3: Full-day Samburu exploration.
  • Day 4: Drive to Mt. Kenya region.
  • Day 5: Lake Nakuru game drive & flamingos.
  • Day 6: Transfer to Maasai Mara, evening safari.
  • Day 7: Morning game drive, return to Nairobi.
